BEE RULES

  1. old slitherlegs will give the word to be spelled along with its definition.
  2. you may ask for the word to be repeated or used in a sentence.
  3. you will pronounce the word, spell it, and pronounce the word again.
  4. you must enunciate each letter clearly so that old slitherlegs can hear it.
  5. if you make a mistake, you can say “excuse me” and start over; this must be said before you complete the word.
  6. old slitherlegs will determine if the word was spelled correctly.
  7. if the correct spelling was given, you will remain in the contest; if the incorrect spelling was given, you will be eliminated from the contest.
  8. the contest will continue until only one speller remains.
  9. old slitherlegs will ask the final three spellers to turn their camera feed on, if they have a camera available to use. he will also ask that your hands be visible on the feed while it is your turn.
